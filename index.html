About Us


The Davis Melee Club is an official club at UC Davis, and we play Super Smash Bros Melee. We have weekly brackets, smash fests, streams, and most importantly: lots of great people. In the future, we plan to have t-shirts, workshops, and large tournaments once or twice each quarter. Be sure to join our Facebook page for easy updates and finding people to play.

Weeklies
Every week we have singles brackets that are free to enter. Come and play some time. There's no reason not to! Weekly brackets also contribute to the seasonal power rankings.

Fests
If you're just looking for a more casual and social place to play melee, head to a local smash fest. Players host sessions multiple times a week, and fests are a great place to improve, whether you're new to the game or a veteran of the community.

Streams
Davis Melee streams on Twitch! Be sure to give the channel a follow and tune in to our stream when Davis hosts larger tournaments. Follow Us on Twitch
